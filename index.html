Venue

The wedding of Sapphire and William will take place on 02 November 2024


The Moat is a Kent farmhouse with a beautiful 14th century barn in the village of Wrotham, which sits along Pilgrim’s Way and along the edge of the North Downs. We love the low beams, Tudor panelling, and open fires – a perfect cosy winter pub environment! For our wedding, we have exclusive access to the barn, attached bar, and a small private garden. The rest of the pub and the main garden will remain open to members of the public, and dogs are welcome in those areas – but not in the wedding! The garden has space heaters, and there are outdoor nooks available for anyone needing a quiet moment. Parking is free, although you will have to enter your registration at the door.

Frequently asked Questions

What is the dress code?

While our dress code is semi-formal, what we really want is for you to wear something that makes you feel confident and ready to party. Don’t feel obliged to shop – we’d love it if you wear something you already own. Dressing to theme is not required, but for those who would like to, our colours are deep green and gold.

Would you like gifts?

We are not opening a registry. For anyone who would like to bring a gift, we would love a thoughtfully chosen Christmas bauble. That way, every year we will be able to reminisce as we decorate. We would also love any contributions to our honeymoon fund, although we stress that this is not expected of anyone.

What is the entertainment?

Throughout the wedding, you will find party games hidden in various locations. Finding these is half the fun! From 8pm, we will have live music in the bar provided by the one and only Five Chambers Full and DJ sets from our favourite No Wave alumni. For those who prefer a quieter evening, we will have board games and art supplies set up in the barn – perfect for kids and adults. We will also have a photo booth set up in the bar throughout the day.
