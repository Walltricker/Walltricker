Details of the Event

The Wedding of William and Sapphire will take place on the 02 November 2024 at the Moat Farmhouse

Venue

The Moat is a Kent farmhouse with a beautiful 14th century barn. It is in the village of Wrotham, which sits along Pilgrim’s Way and along the edge of the North Downs. We love the low beams, Tudor panelling, and open fires – a perfect cosy winter pub environment! For our wedding, we have exclusive access to the barn, attached bar, and a small private garden. The rest of the pub and the main garden will remain open to members of the public, and dogs are welcome in those areas – but not in the wedding! The garden has space heaters, and there are outdoor nooks available for anyone needing a quiet moment. Parking is free, although you will have to enter your registration at the door.

We have a block of rooms reserved at the Holiday Inn Sevenoaks, just a 15-minute walk or 2-minute drive from the venue. To book one of these rooms, please call 01732 781500 and quote the reference ‘H82’. If there is still demand after these rooms are booked, we will look into booking a further block – please let us know if you would like a room but the block is full.

Entertainment

Throughout the wedding, you will find party game ideas hidden in various locations. Finding these is half the fun! From 8pm, we will have live music in the bar provided by the one and only Five Chambers Full and DJ sets from our favourite No Wave alumni. For those who prefer a quieter evening, we will have board games and art supplies set up in the barn – perfect for kids and adults. We will also have a photo booth set up in the bar throughout the day.
