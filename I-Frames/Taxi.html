Holiday Inn

We have a block of rooms reserved at the Holiday Inn Sevenoaks, just a 15-minute walk or 2-minute drive from the venue.

To book one of these rooms, please call 01732 781500 and quote the reference ‘H82’.

If there is still demand after these rooms are booked, we will look into booking a further block – please let us know if you would like a room but the block is full.
